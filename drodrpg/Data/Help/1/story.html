The Story

Tendry was not having a good day. He'd marched several miles underneath the surface of the Eighth with his army to staunch the expanding threat of the Underground Empire. There, despite the words of Beethro Budkin, a professional smitemaster from Dugandy who had come to warn them, his batallion went into battle against a superior force and was routed.

For most soldiers, this meant certain defeat. But Tendry was no ordinary soldier. He was a member of the Stalwart Army from the grand city of Tueno, and that meant only one thing. He could take a butt-kicking with the best of them and sing about it afterwards.

Meeting up again later with the notorious Beethro only served to increase Tendry's resolve to not give up. Beethro thought Stalwarts inexperienced in delving into the Beneath. But what a Stalwart lacked in experience, he could make up in heart. He would show them. He would show Beethro and the Imperials that Stalwarts are not to be taken lightly without a second look. And maybe...just maybe...Tendry would even live to tell the tale. But in the meantime, Tendry will tell his tale to you.

Join Tendry the Stalwart as he sets out on his adventure, and help him live to tell the tale of his grand exploits in the Great Beneath.
